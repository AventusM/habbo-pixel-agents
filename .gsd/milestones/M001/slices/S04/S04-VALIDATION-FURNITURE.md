## Frame Naming Convention

Per Phase 3 research and 04-RESEARCH.md:
- Pattern: `{name}_{size}_{layer}_{direction}_{frame}`
- Example: `chair_64_a_0_0`
  - `chair` = furniture name
  - `64` = sprite size (64×64px for office furniture)
  - `a` = layer (base layer, some furniture has b/c overlays)
  - `0` = direction (0=NE, 2=SE, 4=SW, 6=NW)
  - `0` = animation frame (0 for static furniture)

## Habbo Direction System

- Direction 0: Front-facing (North-East)
- Direction 2: Right-facing (South-East)
- Direction 4: Back-facing (South-West) — **horizontal mirror of direction 2**
- Direction 6: Left-facing (North-West) — **horizontal mirror of direction 0**

Atlas only needs directions 0 and 2 — directions 4 and 6 are generated by horizontal flip at render time.
